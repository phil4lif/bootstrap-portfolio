About Me

Originally from Milwaukee, Wisconsin, I moved to Phoenix in April 2018. After nearly 20 years in the food and beverage industry, even though I love it, I am ready for a change. I will be finishing the University of Arizona Code Bootcamp in May of 2020, I want to start a career in web and app development, so I may use my creative side along with my new technical skills to make beautiful and profitable websites and apps for companies on the cutting edge of service and technology. In my free time I enjoy playing music, I am a bassist and guitarist. I also am a sound engineer, I use both Pro Tools and I have a vintage tape studio. I am often out watching a band at one of several Phoenix music venues, or trying a new restaurant with my amazing girlfriend, Samantha. I also like to spend time in the great outdoors with my adopted shelter dogs, Navi and Link. I am a fan of science fiction and fantasy literature, and I like to watch movies of all types, but I lean towards independent films..
